Our lead singer Steven just adopted a retired greyhound!

The lead singer of "The Blades" Steven is known to have a love for animals. He is mostly seen around the streets of dublin walking his two Huskies. Steven's passion for dogs has led him to donating almost half a million euros to DogsTrust where he helped provide new homes for hundreds of canines as well as providing equipment and food to those dogs that are currently still in care. Of course Steven is always looking to add new members to his furry family including the recently rescued Greyhound Pippin. Pippin was rescued from a dog shelter in belfast where he lived for nearly 6 months until he was taken into care by Steven.
