About Us

The Blades have sold over 2 million copies of their first album "Sharper Than Blades" in Ireland and have currently sold 4 million copies of their new album "Happy Souls" world wide. Their song "Blast" was the 3rd best song on officialcharts.com in 2020 which helped them gain popularity in other countries. The Blades have went on 2 all Ireland tours in 2020 and 2021 with more tours to come in the future and possibly an all Europe tour sometime in summer of 2023.

Chris always enjoyed playing guitar which in the end got him playing concerts all over Ireland. He began playing on an accoustic guitar when he was 7 years old and followed his passion for music in secondary school where he attended a music club where he met his three best friends. Chris easily managed to convince his friends to start their own band and spent their weekends together practicing

Sean spent most of his days helping his dad around their farm and never was the type of person to have his life planned out ahead of time so it definitly was a surprise when he told his family that he was leaving the farm to go chase his new found dream of playing in a rock band. Sean knew that he couldn't spend all his life working on a farm and so he reached for the stars

Steven took a great liking to music when he was attending secondary school. When he first joined the music club he felt bored of constantly playing the tin whistle or the cow bell but when he met Chris and tried playing on an electric guitar his whole perspective on music changed. After many years of practicing he felt like he had mastered the electric guitar and he was ready to be a rock star

Liam had two hobbies, fixing up his old broken down car and playing the drums. For him nothing could beat the feeling of a wrench in one hand and beer in the other. He only had two options in life, be a mechanic or a rock star. Fortunately his friends convinced him to join their brand new band they named "The Blades" and Liam has absolutely no regrets
